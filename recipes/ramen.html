Tonkotsu Ramen

Tonkotsu ramen is a broth made of pure pork. Boiled down to create a rich and thick broth combined seasonings that give a rich and umami flavored noodle soup. Here's what you'll need to make this delicious

  • Pork feet

  • Water

  • Soy sauce

  • Dashi powder

  • Lard

  • Ramen noodles

Steps to making the dish

  1. Heat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Roast pork bones for two hours
  3. Boil a pot of water
  4. Boil roasted bones and pork feet in pot for eight hours
  5. Heat lard in a small pot
  6. Strain bones out of pot
  7. Mix dashi and soy sauce in a bowl
  8. Add lard into bowl
  9. Pour 300cc of broth into bowl
  10. Boil noodles
  11. Make ramen
